Dataset contains the following columns; number (patient identifier), days (number of days of measurements), 
gender (1 or 2 for female or male), age (age in age groups), afftype (1: bipolar II, 2: unipolar depressive, 3: bipolar I), 
melanch (1: melancholia, 2: no melancholia), inpatient (1: inpatient, 2: outpatient), edu (education grouped in years), 
marriage (1: married or cohabiting, 2: single), work (1: working or studying, 2: unemployed/sick leave/pension), 
madrs1 (MADRS score when measurement started), madrs2 (MADRS when measurement stopped).
